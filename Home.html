WHAT IS AGRICULTURE

Agriculture is the science and practice of cultivating the soil,growing and harvesting crops and rearing animals with the overall aim production food and raw materials for the use of human beings.

IMPORTANT OF AGRICULTURE IN NATIONAL ECONOMIY

Agriculture and its development are very important to the economy of a nation because of the reasons:

  1. Agriculture provides food

    2. Food is absolutely assential to life.Without food,life on earth will gradually cease. Various kind of crops are grown to provide essential food materials.Livestock is reared to provide meat,milk and eggs.Fishes and other water-dwelling species are also reared for food.The provision of food is a basic responsibility of agriculture.A well-fed population is energetic,economically productive and enjoys better heath. This result in the creation of greater national wealth.
