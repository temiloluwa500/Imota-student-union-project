WHAT IS AGRICULTURE

Agriculture is the science and practice of cultivating the soil,growing and harvesting crops and rearing animals with the overall aim production food and raw materials for the use of human beings.

IMPORTANT OF AGRICULTURE IN NATIONAL ECONOMIY

Agriculture and its development are very important to the economy of a nation because of the reasons:

  1. Agriculture provides food

    2. Food is absolutely assential to life.Without food,life on earth will gradually cease. This result in the creation of greater national wealth.

  2. Agriculture provide material for clothing and footwear

    3. Good cloths and shoes meet the need of human beings for covering their bodies, protection from unfavourable weather elements and fashion.

    4. medicine and medicinal materials contribute very significantly towards ensuring that human beings live in good health. these medicines and materials are largely derived from plant and animals.Through agriculture, these materials are produced in desired quality and quantity by carefully cultivating plants and rearing animals in large numbers.

SOCIAL VICES

Vices are forms of evil, wicked and criminal actions or behaviours in the society. Vices are social problems and have been thought of as social situations that a large number of observers feel are inappropriate and need remedying. Vices are those acts and conditions that violate societal norms and values.

What are the type of social vices

Some of the social vices include but not limited to examination malpractice, indecent dressing and prostitution, political thuggery, cultism, drug addiction, targeted assassinations, armed robbery, pipe-line vandalism, vandalism of electricity installations and cyber crimes.

What are the causes of social vices

Peer pressure is a major cause of vices in the society. Another cause of social vices is negligence by the parents or a broken home. Most children that engage themselves in vices are from broken homes. Lack of parental care, supervision and attention can make a child engage in social vices.
